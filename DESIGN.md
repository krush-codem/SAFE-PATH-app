# SafePath Design System

## Color Strategy: Committed
We use a deep, sophisticated dark palette with a strong accent color to guide the user's eye and signify safety/action.

### Palette (OKLCH)
- **Background**: `oklch(15% 0.01 250)` (Deep Obsidian Blue-Tinted)
- **Surface**: `oklch(20% 0.02 250)` (Elevated Surface)
- **Primary (Safety)**: `oklch(65% 0.2 250)` (Electric Electric Blue)
- **Secondary (Alert)**: `oklch(60% 0.25 25)` (Urgent Crimson)
- **Success**: `oklch(70% 0.15 150)` (Emerald Green)
- **Neutral High**: `oklch(95% 0.01 250)` (High-visibility Ivory)
- **Neutral Low**: `oklch(40% 0.02 250)` (Muted Silver)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Manrope (W900 for emphasis, W700 for titles)
- **Body**: Inter (W500 for readability)
- **Scale**: 1.25 ratio

## Elevation & Depth
- Avoid traditional box shadows. Use subtle borders and background tints to define layers.
- "Glassmorphism" is reserved for persistent overlays or critical alerts, not as a general card style.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Large, high-contrast, rounded corners (8-12px).
- **Inputs**: Solid background with subtle borders that highlight on focus.
- **Cards**: Flat with a 1px border `oklch(25% 0.02 250)`.
